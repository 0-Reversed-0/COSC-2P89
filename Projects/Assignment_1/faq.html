What is the difference between open-back and closed-back headphones?

Open-back headphones have ear cups that let air and sound pass through, creating a more natural and spacious sound — perfect for home listening or studio work. Closed-back headphones, on the other hand, block out external noise and prevent sound from leaking, making them ideal for commuting, travel, or use in noisy environments.
